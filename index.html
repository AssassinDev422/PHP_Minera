FAQ's

What is HashPower?

Cryptocurrency mining power (HashPower)  is rated on a scale of Hashes per second. Every time a miner successfully solves a block, a new hash is created. A hash algorithm turns this large amount of transaction data into a fixed-length hash.

Like a code, if you know the algorithm you can solve a hash and get the original transaction data out.  But to the ordinary eye, it's just a bunch of numbers crammed together and remains practically impossible to get the original data out.

What about Power?

Cryptocurrency mining trades electrical/physical power to digital currency.  Mining machines require a significant amount of power. Cryptocurrency mining uses more energy then 159 countries .
